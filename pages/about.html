ID & UX
Designer



Formed as an Industrial Designer in Lima, Peru, Gerardo had the opportunity to collaborate with multipe disciplines from early in his career. Through moving across multidisciplinary teams he realised the crucial role that design plays in achieving innovative outcomes. This as a result of exploring scenarios where designers are invited to step out of their comfort zone.

His inquisitive nature as designer has allowed him to deepen his knowledge in human-centred design, co-design and co-creation giving him the opportunity and first hand experience of working closely with users. Through his career he has had the opportunity of collaborating with artists, designers, architects, engineers and scientists addressing subjects such as spatial design, sports perfomance, mobility and health.

After living and working in Lima and Barcelona Gerardo settled in London, UK, where he now resides as a UX & Industrial Designer focusing in products and services that tackle healthcare innovation.
